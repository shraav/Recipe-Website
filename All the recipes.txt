  <h1> BAKED BEAN WRAPS</h1>
  <h4> INGREDIENTS </h4>
  <p>4 whole wheat rotis.</p>
  <p>1 cup tinned baked beans & 2 medium onions.</p>
  <p>1 tablespoon oil, 1 medium green capsicum, chopped 5-6 garlic cloves, thinly sliced.</p>
  <p>3 tablespoons chopped fresh coriander. Few ice-berglettuce leaves, shredded.</p>
  <p>Tomato ketchup as required & red chilli sauce.</p>
  <p>4 tablespoons og grated processed cheese.</p>
  <h4> METHOD </h4>
  <p>Finely chop one onion and slice the other. Heat the oil in a non-stick pan, add the chooped onion,capsicum and garlic,and saute for a few minutes.</p>
  <p>Add the baked beans and saute for a while.A dd the chopped coriander and mix. Place the rotis on a flat surface. Spread a little tomato ketchup and red chilli sauce on each one.</p>
  <p>Place some of the baked beans mixture at one end of each roti.Sprinkle the sliced onion,lettuce and grated cheese over the baked beans and roll up.</p>
  <p>Cut each roll into diagonal pieces and serve, or wrap rach roll in an aluminium foil and serve.</p>

  <h1> BREAD PAKORE </h1>
  <h4> INGREDIENTS </h4>
  <p>250 grams fresh gram kernels.</p>
  <p>2 teaspoons green chilli paste.</p>
  <p>1 teaspoon ginger paste and chaat masala.</p>
  <p>1 teaspoon coriander powder.</p>
  <p>2 tablespoons chopped fresh coriander.</p>
  <p>5-6 tablespoons gram flour.</p>
  <p>Salt to taste & oil foe deep frying.</p>
  <h4> METHOD </h4>
  <p>Coarsely grind the corn kernels.</p>
  <p>Place the ground corn in a large bowl. Add the green chilli paste, ginger paste, chaat masala, coriander poeder, chopped coriander, gram flour and salt.Mix well.</p>
  <p>Heat sufficient oil in a kadai.Drop small portions of the corn mixture into the hot oil and deep-fry the pakore till golden brown on both sides.Drain on absorbent paper.</p>
  <p>Serve hot with green chutney or ketchup.</p>

  <h1> COCONUT BURFI </h1>
  <h4> INGREDIENTS </h4>
  <p>2 cups grated fresh coconut.</p>
  <p>1 cup sugar and 1/2 cup fresh ice-cream.</p>
  <p>A pinch of green cardamom powder & 2 tablespoons og powdered sugar.</p>
  <p>1/2 tablespoon of ghee.</p>
  <h4> METHOD </h4>
  <p>Heat the nin-stick pan on medium heat. Add the coconut,sugar,cream & cardamom and cook stirring continuously for 15 minutes or till the mixture turns golden brown.</p>
  <p>Remove from the heat & add the powdered sugar and mix. </p>
  <p>Grease a thali with ghee and pour in the coconut mixture & spread evenly. </p>
  <p>Cool and cut into diamonds or squares and serve.</p>

  <h1> CRUSTY POTATO FINGERS </h1>
  <h4><b> INGREDIENTS </b></h4>
  <p> 2 large potatoes</p>
  <p> Salt to taste </p>
  <p> 2 cups cornflakes crushed</p>
  <p> Oil for deep-frying </p>

  <h4><b> BATTER </b></h4>
  <p> 1 Onion roughly chopped </p>
  <p> 1 Green chilli and 1/2 inch ginger chopped</p>
  <p> Salt to taste </p>
  <p> 1/2 Cup refined flour and 1 Tablespoon cornflour</p>

  <h4><i><b> METHOD </b></i></h4>
  <ol>
    <li> For the batter,grind the onion,green chilli,ginger and salt to a fine paste.</li>
    <li> Mix the ground paste with the flour and corn flour. Add 2 table-spoons of water to make a thick batter. </li>
    <li> Peel and cut the potatoes length ways into even strips. Heat 4 cups of water in a non-stock pan. Add the salt and parboil the sliced potatoes.Drain and set aside.</li>
    <li> Heat sufficient oil in a kadai. Dip the potatoes in the batter,roll in crushed corn flakes and deep fry till golden-brown.Drain on absorbent paper.</li>
    <li> Serve hot,with sauce or chutney</li>
  </ol>

  <h1> MAWA CUPCAKES </h1>
  <h4> INGREDIENTS </h4>
  <p>1 tablespoon mawa crumbled.150 grams butter softened.</p>
  <p>3/4 cups powdered sugar & 5 tablespoons milk powder.</p>
  <p>1 cup refined flour, 2 tablespoons cornflour.</p>
  <p>1 teaspoon baking powder & soda bicarbonate.</p>
  <p>1/4 spoon of green cardamom powder, 1/4 cup buttermilk & 2 tablespoons milk.</p>
  <h4> METHOD </h4>
  <p>Preheat the oven to 180 deg C.Grease 12 oulds or paper cups.</p>
  <p>Cream the butter and powdered sugar in a bowl.Sift the milk powder ointo bowl and continue to mix.Add the mawa and mix again.</p>
  <p>Sift the flour, cornflour, baking powder, soda bicarbonate and cardamom powder together into the bowl and mix.</p>
  <p>Add buttermilk to adjust the consistency.If the batter is still too thick add a little milk and mix.</p>
  <p>Pour the batter into the greased moulds or paper cups.Tap the moulds to level the batter.Bake for 30 minutes.</p>
  <p>Remove from the oven and place on a wire rack to cool before unmoulding.</p>
  <p>Serve warm or cold.</p>

  <h1> SPROUT CUTLET </h1>
  <h4><b> INGREDIENTS </b></h4>
  <p> 1/2 cup sprouted white peas,boiled. </p>
  <p> 1/2 cup sprouted brown bengal gram,boiled. </p>
  <p> 1/2 cup sprouted green bengal gram, boiled. </p>
  <p> 2 tablespoons finely chopped fresh coriander. </p>
  <p> salt to taste. </p>
  <p> 1/2 teaspoon red chilli powder and bread crumbs. </p>
  <p> 1/2 teaspoon chaat masala and cumin powder. </p>
  <p> oil foe shallow-frying. </p>
  <h4><b> METHOD </b></h4>
  <p> Combine the chopped coriander, white peas, brown bengal gram, green begal gram and salt in a bowl. Mash well with a potato masher.</p>
  <p> Add the red chilli powder, chaat masala and cumin powder and mix well. Heat a little oil in a non-stick pan.</p>
  <p> Divide the sprout mixture into eight portions and shape them into small cutlets, roll them in bread crumbs and place on the pan.</p>
  <p> Shallow-fry till the underside is done, flip over and cook till both the sides are evenly gold. </p>
  <p> Serve hot with tomato ketchup and green chutney./p>

  <h1> VEGETABLE ROLLS </h1>
  <h4> INGREDIENTS </h4>
  <p>10-12 French Beans finely chopped.</p>
  <p>2 medium carrots finely chopped.</p>
  <p>2 large potatoes finely chopped.</p>
  <p>2 tablespoon oil + for deep frying.</p>
  <p>1/2 teaspoon of garam masala powder.</p>
  <p>1 1/2 teaspoon of chilli powder and dried mango powder.</p>
  <p>4 bread slices and salt to taste.</p>
  <h4>METHOD</h4>
  <p>Heat 2 tablespoons of oil in a non-stick lan and saute the french beans, carrots and potatoes till all the moisture evaporates and the vegetables are slightly tender.</p>
  <p>Mash them slightly while they are hot.Add the garam masala powder, chilli powder, dried mango powder and salt.Mix them well.</p>
  <p>Soak the bread slices in water for a couple of seconds and then sqeeze ouit thoroughly. Add to the vegetables.</p>
  <p>Coat the rolls with the semolina and deep-fry over medium heat till golden brown and crisp.</p>
  <p>Serve hot with ketchup or green chutney.</p>
